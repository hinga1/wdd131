Busia Shamba Community Garden Site Plan

Site Name

The site name is Busia Shamba Community Garden. This name was chosen to reflect the primary focus of the site, which is to promote and provide information about the community garden initiative in our local area. The name is straightforward and descriptive, ensuring it is easily understood by potential visitors.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as an informational hub for the local community garden initiative. The site will provide details about the garden, instructions on how to join, a calendar of upcoming events, gardening tips, and various resources for members. The goal is to promote sustainability, healthy living, and community engagement through gardening.

Scenarios

  • What is the mission of the Local Community Garden Initiative?
  • How can I become a member of the community garden?
  • What are the benefits of joining the community garden?
  • When is the next community gardening event?
  • Where can I find tips and tutorials on gardening?

Color Schema

The selected color scheme for the website includes the following colors:

  • Green (#4CAF50) - Used for headings and accents to represent growth and nature.
  • Beige (#F5F5DC) - Used for the background to provide a neutral and calm feel.

Typography

The selected fonts for the website are:

  • Open Sans - Used for body text due to its readability and clean appearance.
  • Merriweather - Used for headings to provide a contrasting, elegant look.
